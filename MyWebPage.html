History and Geography


The Province of Ontario is the most populated province in Canada
and the second largest one in total area after Quebec.
It is located in East-Central Canada, surrounded by the Province of Quebec in east,
Manitoba in west, by Hudson Bay and James Bay in north and USA in south.
The history of Ontario covers a period of thousands of years, started with the arrival of thePaleo-Indians.....
